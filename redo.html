Welcome to TailoRite

Whenever needed we clean and alter all your garments to meet your personal standards and requirements. Have any rips, tears, holes, buttons? We can fix it! Have a stubborn coffee stain? We can get it out!

TailoRite is committed to using environmentally safe solvents to clean and maintain your precious garments. Our green-machine utilizes a eco-friendly solvent that has no toxins or adverse chemicals. Our petroleum based solvent used in our dry-cleaning process gives us the ability to remove day-to-day dirt and grime from your clothing. Our wet-cleaning system allows special enzymes and stain removing agents to remove tough stains from your cottons, linens, denim, etc. while still maintaining the look and luster of your clothing. This ensures that you and your family are not being exposed to any hazardous or harmful solvents that may still be lingering in your garments when you receive them.

Through thorough inspection processes, we are able to ensure our customers that their garments are virtually spotless and well-finished. We are able to remove 99% of stains on the first attempt. We pledge to work out all stain issues until we find a resolution. We pride ourselves on delivering a high-quality garment each and every time you utilize our services.

No matter the issue, TailoRite has the answer that fits you.

In the Beginning...

Joseph Caldwell Sr. began TailoRite Complete Clothing Care with the help of three other partners in 1956. All interested parties were trained at the Metropolitan School of Tailoring to service the tailoring and alteration needs of its south-side clients. In 1973, Joseph Caldwell decided to venture out on his own and incorporate a dry-cleaning aspect to his service scope. Since that time, TailoRite Complete Clothing Care has been the pioneer of the industry when it comes to garment care services. Our goal is to continue to educate ourselves in the textile world in order to always provide our supporting communities upper echelon service.
