Welcome to TailoRite!

TailoRite is a family owned Dry Cleaners that has been around for over 60 years. With this experience we have in garment care, we are able to remove 99% of stains on the first attempt. We strive to continue to work on the issue until we find a resolution.We pride ourselves on delivering a high-quality garment each and every time you utilize our services. Through thorough inspection processes, we are able to ensure our customers that their garments are virtually spotless and well-finished. That is our pledge to give value-added service.

High Standards

TailoRite Complete Clothing Care is a Drycleaning and Laundry Institute Member. We are the first to get new information about the garments that are being sold in the retail world. Whether it be the newest dress shirt or the latest designer dress, DLI gives us the information that we need to process those garments in the most efficient and effective process. TailoRite and its fifty plus years of experience can address anything that your wardrobe calls for!

Services

Alterations

If you have gained or lost weight, don't discard those old clothes. Let our experienced tailors bring your wardrobe back to life by altering your clothing to fit the new you. Just stop by one of our three convenient locations and be custom-fitted by one our trained staff. We can custom fit that garment so it compliments your specific body type. Let TailoRite help you better your image while still falling within your personal budget. TailoRite wants nothing more than to see it's clients happy and leaving with excellent service.

Laundry

Our silicone based starching system is the industry standard. We use starches that don't change the look of the garment, but still give that hard finish our customers are looking for. Our wet-cleaning system is a non-aggressive process that utilizes chemicals that enables us to maintain the color of our denims and cottons. This also extends the life of your washable items and preserves the original look over time. We are committed to keeping all your laundry items clean, crisp, and renewed.

Dry Cleaning

TailoRite is committed to using environmentally safe solvents to clean and maintain your precious garments. Our "green-machine" utilizes a "Eco-friendly" solvent that has no toxins or adverse chemicals. This ensures that you and your family are not being exposed to any hazardous or harmful solvents that may still be lingering in your garments when you receive them. Our petroleum based solvent used in our dry-cleaning process gives us the ability to remove day-to-day dirt and grime from your clothing. Our wet-cleaning system allows special enzymes and stain removing agents to remove tough stains from your cottons, linens, denim, etc. while still maintaining the look and luster of your clothing.
